About Us

Kekoil is a premier UK-based trading enterprise, expertly sourcing and managing the international flow of industrial oil and gas products, alongside vital agricultural and food commodities. We bridge continents, connecting reliable suppliers with discerning international buyers.

Our operations span Europe, North Africa, the Middle East, and Turkey, with a sharp focus on the energy sector, trading essential industrial oil and gas products and equipment. In parallel, we are deeply involved in the agricultural domain, sourcing premium olive oil and other Mediterranean delights directly from producers.

Leveraging robust regional relationships across North Africa, the Middle East, and Turkey, Kekoil stands as a steadfast intermediary. We empower businesses by granting them access to superior products and resilient supply chains, fostering growth and opportunity in every transaction.
